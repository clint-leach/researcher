About me

I am a PhD candidate in ecology working in Colleen Webb’s lab at Colorado State University. Broadly, I am interested in ecological networks and their application in food webs and disease ecology. More specifically, I am interested in coupling mechanistic and statistical models to make inference about the structure of the network processes driving community and/or disease dynamics.
